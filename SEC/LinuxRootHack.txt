How to change a forgotten password.

1. I have a Linux computer.

2. I do not know the password.

3. I am running LiveCD with, for example, Slack.

4. I mount the disk I want to get.

5. I change my root permissions

6. I use chroot

where path is the location of the "shadow" file from the newly mounted drive
